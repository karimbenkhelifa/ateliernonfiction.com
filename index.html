About

Atelier Non-Fiction is a boutique production and consulting company founded in Berlin by Karim Ben Khelifa.

We make work that is hard to categorize and easy to remember. Our practice spans kinetic installation, responsive environments, spatial sound, and extended reality.

We conceptualize, develop, and produce original projects, and bring our process and expertise to external collaborations at any stage, from early concept through to delivery.

Based in Berlin. Working internationally.
